Tic-Tac-Toe-Shirts

Project Qonnect


We are also busy on a project we named “Project Qonnect” and we developed an app for scanning, and creating qr codes (download app from Google playstore – QR Code Qonnect), entrepreneurs and anyone can download it on google playstore and start creating their own qr codes and to scan other qr codes on the go, and to make it even more useful, the app also has voice search for on the go internet browsing. We also have created a website where people and businesses can go and create or generate QR Codes, we also teach more on the site about how to use qr codes for business and marketing to grow your company as well as offering a more custom or dynamic qr codes (this are qr codes that with contents that can be changed or edited from time to time if needed, the qr code is not static, but dynamic) the website is www.qrqonnect.co.za. This project goes hand in hand with the QR coded t-shirts we created for marketing purposes. The T-shirts are printed the individual, artist or company name, what they do and also a QR code directing people who scans it to a landing page with more info and details about the person or business including links also to their projects etc. social media handles and website.
